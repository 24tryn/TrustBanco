Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Trust Banco ("Company," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our financial services platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, date of birth)
  • Address and financial information
  • Account credentials and security questions
  • Transaction history and account activity
  • Communication preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send promotional communications and updates
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud and security issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Law enforcement when legally required
  • Regulatory authorities for compliance purposes

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and hold certain information. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of data
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications
