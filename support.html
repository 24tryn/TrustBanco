Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my PIN? expand_more

If you've forgotten your PIN, you can select "Forgot Passcode?" on the sign-in page. You'll need to answer your security questions to proceed with recovery. For additional security, you may also contact our support team.

How long do transfers take? expand_more

Most transfers within Trust Banco complete instantly. External transfers typically process within 1-3 business days depending on the receiving bank. Wire transfers may take 1-2 business days.

Is my account secure? expand_more

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your account is protected by a 6-digit PIN and optional biometric authentication. Never share your login credentials with anyone.

How do I update my account information? expand_more

You can update most account information in Settings. For changes to sensitive information like your SSN or address, please contact our support team for verification purposes.

What should I do if I notice unauthorized activity? expand_more

Contact us immediately at 1-800-TRUSTBANCO or email security@trustbanco.com. Lock your account and change your PIN right away. We'll investigate the issue and take appropriate action.
